Let's bring back the HRE!

For the sake of a better world the Holy Roman Empire must be returned and central Europe balkanized. By signing this petition you will aid us in our cause of restoring this old, glorious empire. When we have reached 50 million signatures we will send an official request to the governments of Denmark, Germany, The Netherlands, Belgium, France, Luxembourg, Austria, Czechia, Liechenstein, Italy, Monaco, Slovenia, Poland, Russia and any other relevant countries regarding the restoration of the empire.
